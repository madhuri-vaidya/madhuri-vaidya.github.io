Who Am I?

Welcome to my little corner of the internet!

I am a Power and Performance engineer with 6+ years of experience shaping the next-gen server CPUs. I analyze how workloads behave and uncover optimization opportunities at both the hardware and software levels.

Specializing in benchmarking, workload analysis, and platform tuning, I bring deep expertise in SoC characterization, CPU architecture, and power management — driving performance gains while improving thermal and energy efficiency.

I'm deeply interested in how subsystems like cores, cache & memory hierarchies, interconnects, and power management units — interact. Diving into these system-level dynamics helps me diagnose bottlenecks and fine-tune performance across diverse workloads.

Skilled in tools like VTune, EMON, and Linux Perf, and fluent in C++, Python, and Bash, I combine hands-on debugging with automation and leadership. I've led teams, mentored engineers, and built tools to streamline benchmarking.
