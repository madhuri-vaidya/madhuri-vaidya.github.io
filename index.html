Who Am I?

Hi! Welcome to my little corner of the internet 🙂

I'm Madhuri Vaidya, a Power and Performance engineer with 7+ years of experience shaping the next-gen server CPUs. I analyze how workloads behave and uncover optimization opportunities at both the hardware and software levels.

Specializing in SoC characterization, CPU architecture, and power management, I drive performance and efficiency by diving deep into the interactions between cores, cache hierarchies, and interconnects. This system-level perspective allows me to diagnose complex bottlenecks and fine-tune performance across diverse workloads to improve both thermal and energy efficiency.

Outside of work, I'm a scientist-turned-artist in the kitchen, where I love exploring different cuisines and mastering new dishes. I am an avid reader, and most rainy weekends find me lost in a good book. When I'm not cooking or reading, you can ususally find me hiking in the mountains or exploring a waterfall.

I hope you enjoy exploring this space. It's where I reflect on my thoughts and share my experiences, both technical and personal. If you would like to reach out or just say hi, let's connect!
